CSV Formatting Instructions

Your CSV must contain an employee_id column (or EmployeeID). Standard alphanumeric IDs are recommended. The system maps columns case-insensitively and handles either snake_case or PascalCase headers.

Required Columns

  • employee_id (String, unique identifier)
  • first_name (String, default: "Employee")
  • last_name (String, default: index/number)

Key ML Features

  • age (Integer)
  • department (Sales, Research & Development, Human Resources)
  • job_role (Job Title)
  • monthly_income (Float/Integer compensation)
  • overtime (Yes / No or True / False)
  • job_satisfaction (1-4 score)
